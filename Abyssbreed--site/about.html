About Us

Descend into the shadows of sound with ABYSSBREED RECORDS, where darkness is not just heard — it is felt. We are a collective of obsessive curators, dedicated to uncovering music that lurks in the depths: black metal shrieks, abyssal synths, and echoes of chaos that stir the soul.

Each playlist is a journey through shadowed realms, a ritual of auditory intensity designed to awaken something primal within. Here, no compromise exists — only the raw, the unrelenting, the underground.

We are drawn to the unknown, to the forgotten corners of music that others fear to tread. Our mission is simple: to summon the audial abyss and guide listeners into its endless depths.

Explore. Listen. Submit to the darkness. Join ABYSSBREED RECORDS and let the abyss breed itself inside you.
